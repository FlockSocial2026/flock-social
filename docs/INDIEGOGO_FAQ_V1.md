# Indiegogo FAQ V1

## Who is Flock Social for?
Members, pastors/staff, and church admins who want unified communication and engagement tools.

## What is already built?
Core social MVP + moderation stack + Flock role/events/announcement foundation + pilot operations assets.

## What happens after this campaign?
We execute pilot cohort rollout, publish weekly KPI updates, and iterate based on real church outcomes.

## Is the product live today?
Core features are built and continuing through pilot-readiness execution; broader launch follows validated pilot outcomes.

## How do you handle fairness for prayer leaderboards?
Leaderboard is disabled by default until anti-gaming controls and moderation safeguards are fully implemented.
