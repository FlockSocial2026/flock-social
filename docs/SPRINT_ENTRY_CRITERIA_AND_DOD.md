# Sprint Entry Criteria & Definition of Done

## Entry Criteria
- Live campaign URL available
- Final perks/pricing values approved
- Final media assets exported and uploaded
- Owner coverage confirmed for first 48h

## Definition of Done (Sprint)
- Launch wave executed with logged evidence
- Outreach targets met or explained with corrective action
- Discovery calls scheduled within target range
- At least one onboarding started
- Week-1 KPI and executive summary published
