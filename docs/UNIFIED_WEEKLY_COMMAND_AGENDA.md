# Unified Weekly Command Agenda

## 1) Campaign Performance (10 min)
- Funding trend
- Channel conversion
- Top objections

## 2) Pilot Operations (15 min)
- Onboarding progression
- Church KPI scorecard review
- At-risk churches and interventions

## 3) Product Delivery (15 min)
- Top friction points from pilot/campaign
- This-week ship targets
- Dependency blockers

## 4) Decisions & Owners (10 min)
- Decision record updates
- Owner assignments
- Due dates

## 5) Risk/Escalation Review (10 min)
- New escalations
- Mitigation status
- SLA exceptions
