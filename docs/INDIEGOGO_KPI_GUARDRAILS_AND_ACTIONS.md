# Indiegogo KPI Guardrails & Actions

| KPI | Guardrail | Trigger Condition | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| Daily funding velocity | >=70% of target | Falls below 70% for 1 day | Increase high-converting channel outreach within 6h |
| Channel conversion rate | No >25% day-over-day drop | Drops >25% | Swap CTA/copy variant and retest |
| Response SLA | <12h average | Exceeds 12h | Enter response sprint and clear backlog |
| Objection repetition | <3 repeats/day per theme | 3+ repeats | FAQ and messaging patch same day |
| Day-1 to Day-7 momentum | Stable or improving | Downward trend 3 consecutive checks | Escalation review + offer/packaging adjustment |
